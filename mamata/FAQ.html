FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

What happens if the order is placed after the cut-off time ?

If the order is placed after the cut off time, you will receive the order next business day.

When an how can I cancel the order ?

Within 24 hours of placing your order you are able to cancel it by calling our customer services at +91 99 1064 4899 Mon-Sat (9am-6pm) or emailing us at cx@giftcart.com, provided that the order has not been shipped. After 24 hours we will not be able to cancel your order.

How do I know my order has been confirmed ?

When you complete an order online, you should see a page showing your order and payment details. Shortly after you complete your order, we will send you an email confirming we have received it.

How do I cheak the current status of my orders ?

When you complete an order online, you should see a page showing your order and payment details. Shortly after you complete your order, we will send you an email confirming we have received it. All registered users can also sign in to “My Account” page on the website and check their recent orders.

How can I become a vendor with Giftcart.com ?

If you would like to become a vendor with Giftcart.com please register your interest here. Our business team will get back to you with a response once they have reviewed your application.

Spoof/Phishing/False Mails?

Giftcart will never ask you to confirm any account or credit card details via email. If you receive an email claiming to be from Giftcart.com asking you to do so, please ignore it and do not respond.
